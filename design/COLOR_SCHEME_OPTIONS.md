## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

1. **Stick to One Scheme**: Don't mix - consistency is key
2. **Test on Real Devices**: Colors look different on phones vs desktop
3. **Check Accessibility**: Use contrast checker (4.5:1 minimum)
4. **Save All Options**: Easy to switch schemes later if you change your mind
5. **Consider Your Logo**: Pick colors that work for app icon too
6. **Think Long-Term**: Will this work for "Space Heist" or "Pirate Heist" chapters?
