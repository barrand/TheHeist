## Visual Theme

All characters feature:
- **Art Style:** Borderlands-inspired 2D illustration

## Character Design Philosophy

Each character is designed to:
1. **Represent their specialty** through clothing and equipment
2. **Show personality** through expression and attitude
3. **Reflect diversity** in appearance and background
4. **Maintain cohesion** through consistent art style
5. **Enable player connection** through relatable characteristics
